MAIN RESULTS:
  • Critique Market: 0.320 ± 0.142 findings/cycle
  • Uniform (baseline): 0.317 ± 0.152 findings/cycle
  • Random (control): 0.194 ± 0.090 findings/cycle
  
  → No significant difference between Critique and Uniform (p=0.968)
  → Both significantly better than Random (p < 0.05)

DOMAIN VARIATION:

  • Social:        0.396 efficiency (high)  
  • Environmental: 0.176 efficiency (low)
  → 2× difference across domains (p=0.026)

SATURATION:

  • Medical: r=-0.978, p=0.022
  • Environmental: r=-0.994, p=0.006
  → Clear diminishing returns with increased budget
